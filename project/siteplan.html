Beginner Workout Tips

Site Name

Beginner Workout Tips

This name directly reflects the purpose of the site—to help beginners start their fitness journey with simple and effective workout tips.

Optional domain availability: beginnerworkouttips.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide essential fitness guidance for beginners, including simple workout routines, nutritional advice, and motivational tips to help users start and stick to a fitness plan.

Scenarios

  • What are some beginner-friendly exercises I can do at home?
  • How can I create a simple workout plan to gain muscle?

Color Schema

The website will use a clean and energetic color palette to motivate and engage users:

  • Primary Color: #28a745 (Green) – Used for headings, buttons, and call-to-action elements.
  • Secondary Color: #f8f9fa (Light Gray) – Used for background and sections to provide contrast.

Typography

The following fonts will be used across the website for readability and energy:

  • Headings: 'Roboto', sans-serif – Bold and clear for section titles.
  • Body Text: 'Open Sans', sans-serif – Easy to read for paragraphs and general content.
